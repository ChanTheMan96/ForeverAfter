Venue Rental Details & Inclusions

Your venue rental includes access from 9:00 AM to 11:00 PM, with last call for alcohol at 10:45 PM. Additional hours can be arranged if needed. The rental comes with a beautifully set ceremony space with chairs, an elegant banquet hall with linens, and private suites for both the bride and groom. Complimentary soda, water, and tea are also included.

Please note that bartender fees, cleaning fees, and day-of-event insurance are not included in the rental price. Additionally, food and alcohol pricing are separate, and a bar minimum is required. Let us know if you have any special requests or need further details to tailor your experience!

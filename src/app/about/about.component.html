About Us

“Unforgettable moments only last seconds but give us the memories that last "Forever After...." -Forever After, Banquet and Event Center was established in 2022, with the idea of becoming one of the most unique, efficient, elegant and affordable venues in the metro East. We are located in Pontoon Beach IL, right accross the Mississippi River. Our venue is operated by management of Uncle Linnys Restaurant which have been in business for over 20 years. Our facility can accommodate up to 350 guests, with a outdoor patio of 8,000 sq feet which is designed for ceremonies and other outdoor activities. Our goal is to create a memorable moments that will last forever and our facility can do just that!! -We hope to see you soon and cannot wait to help plan your “Forever After” event.
